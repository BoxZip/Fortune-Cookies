Fortune Cookie

A crispy cookie shell, concealing a secret message.

What does your future have in store?

Fortune Cookie On-Chain NFT is an open edition with a supply of 1,000,000 tokens.

Fortune Cookies are On-Chain Non-Fungible Tokens, which means their data is hosted completely on the blockchain. They don't require IPFS or a HTTP/S webserver to provide hosting for the metadata and image files.

Fortune Cookies are minted with a secret message hidden inside them.

The message is hidden until the owner of the NFT opens the cookie to reveal what is inside.

You can only open and read the Fortune Cookies that you own.

Fortune Cookies can be transfered after being opened; or before, while the mystery remains intact.

Minting is paused.

Fortune Cookies are only available on the Polygon Network.
